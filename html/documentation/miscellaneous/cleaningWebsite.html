How to clean up old Allsky files from a Website on your Pi

You can easily limit the number of days of images stored on your local Website by using the WebUI to edit the WEB_DAYS_TO_KEEP setting in config.sh with the number of days' data you want to keep. Older files will be removed every morning.

How to clean up old Allsky files from a remote Website

The following instructions assume your startrails, keograms, and timelapse files are stored in the directories specified in ftp-settings.sh.

Because remote web sites vary, the instructions below are generic and it's assumed you know how to perform the steps.

  • Connect to the web site.
  • For each of the startrails, keograms, and videos directories do the following:
    • Enter the directory.
    • Search for all *.jpg, *.png, or *.mp4 files older than your threshold, for example, older than one month.
    • Remove them. Do NOT remove the index.php files!
    • Enter the thumbnails sub-directory and delete files older than your threshold.
